MEANING: 
a round fruit with red, yellow, or green skin and firm white flesh
This ia a Noun.

FULL DEFINITION:
1: the fleshy usually rounded red, yellow, or green edible pome fruit of a
    usually cultivated tree (genus Malus) of the rose family; 
    also :  an    apple tree — compare crab apple.
2: a fruit (as a star apple) or other vegetative growth (as an oak apple)    
    suggestive of an apple.

IDIOM:
apple of one's eye
one that is highly cherished <his daughter is the apple of his eye>
