MEANING:
: a statement in which you express too much pride in yourself or in something you have, have done, or are connected to in some way
: a reason to be proud : something impressive that someone or something has or has done

It is a noun.

FULL DEFINTION:
1:  the act or an instance of boasting :  brag
2:  a cause for pride

EXAMPLE:
When he says he's the richest man in town, he's not just making an idle boast

SYNONYMS:
glory, credit, crown jewel, honor, jewel, pride, treasure, trophy

ANTONYMS:
disgrace, dishonor; blemish, blot, defect, shame, slur, smirch, smudge, stain, stigma; eyesore, fright, horror, mess
