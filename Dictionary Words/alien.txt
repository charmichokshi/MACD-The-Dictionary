MEANING:
from another country.
too different from something to be acceptable or suitabl.
This is a Adjective. 

FULL DEFINITION:
1 a : belonging or relating to another person, place, or thing :  strange
  b : relating, belonging, or owing allegiance to another country or             government : foreign
2 : differing in nature or character typically to the point of     incompatibility

EXAMPLE:
New immigrants with customs alien to the community where they have settled.

SYNONYMS:
foreign, nonnative

ANTONYMS:
domestic, native
