MEANING: 
:  the mature female of cattle (genus Bos)
: the mature female of various usually large animals (as an elephant, whale, or moose)
This ia a Noun.

EXAMPLE:
The cows need to be milked twice a day.

OTHER MAMMALS TERMS:
dormouse, dugong, gibbon, grimalkin, sable, stoat, ungulate, vole

ORIGIN AND ETYMOLOGY OF COW:
Middle English cou, from Old English cu; akin to Old High German kuo cow,
Latin bos head of cattle, Greek bous, Sanskrit go.

First Known Use: before 12th century
