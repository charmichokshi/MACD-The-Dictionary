MEANING:
no longer alive or living;  no longer having life
this is an adjective

FULL DEFINITION:
1)  lacking power or effect <a dead law> (2) :  no longer having interest, relevance, or significance <a dead issue>

2)  no longer in use :  obsolete <a dead language>

3)  no longer active :  extinct <a dead volcano>

4)  lacking in gaiety or animation 

EXAMPLES:
He was found dead in his apartment yesterday.
Our legs were completely dead after hiking all day.
