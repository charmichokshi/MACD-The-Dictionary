MEANING:
a period of 24 hours beginning at midnight; one of the seven time periods that make up a week
this is a noun

FULL DEFINITION:
1) a :  the time of light between one night and the next 
    b :  daylight 
    c :  daytime

2) the period of rotation of a planet (as earth) or a moon on its axis

3) the mean solar day of 24 hours beginning at mean midnight

4) the time established by usage or law for work, school, or business

EXAMPLES:
That was the happiest day of my life.
She works eight hours a day.
