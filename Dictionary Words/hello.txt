MEANING:
:the act of saying the word hello to someone as a greeting
This is Noun.

FULL DEFINITION:
: an expression or gesture of greeting -used interjectionally in greeting, 
  in answering the telephone, or to express surprise

EXAMPLE:
They welcomed us with a warm hello.

SYNONYMS:
greeting, salutation, welcome

ANTONYMS:
farewell, good-bye, bon voyage
