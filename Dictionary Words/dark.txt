MEANING:
having very little or no light
this is an adjective

FULL DEFINITION:
1) a :  devoid or partially devoid of light :  not receiving, reflecting, transmitting, or radiating light <a dark room>
    b :  transmitting only a portion of light <dark glasses>

2) a :  not clear to the understanding
    b :  not known or explored because of remoteness <the darkest reaches of the continent>

3) possessing depth and richness <a dark voice>

EXAMPLES:
She sat in the dark room alone.
Dark clouds of smoke were coming from the windows.
