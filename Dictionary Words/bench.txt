MEANING:
a: a long and usually hard seat for two or more people
the bench law 
b: the place where a judge sits in a court of law
c: the position or rank of a judge

It is a noun.

FULL DEFINITION:
a :  a long seat for two or more persons
b :  a thwart in a boat
c (1) :  a seat on which the members of an athletic team await a turn or opportunity to play (2) :  the reserve players on a team; broadly :  a reserve force

EXAMPLE:
The lawyer asked if he could approach the bench.

SYNONYMS:
adjudicator, beak [chiefly British], judge, court, jurist, justice, magistrate
